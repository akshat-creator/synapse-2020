Dear Partipant,

1 Day To Go

1. The event will take place on Discord (either on a browser at discord.com, or on the app).Please ensure that you have joined the general EvolvePD server and your main ER server.Teams are allowed to use any resources they have to research (primarily; Google). 

2. Each team's members need to have the Doctor and Team Name role on their ER servers to have access to the questions as well as to be able to communicate with the patient. ( please refer to the previous mail for directions on how to get these roles)

3. Synapse Members will take over the role of the patient, hospital staff and administration for the event. Teams can try to deduce the disease by interacting with the patient, asking questions about symptoms and asking for tests in the channel which is named after their team. The teamname channel is the main communication channel between the Patient and Doctor.

4. For EvolvePD'20 we have made a bot to cut down on wait time and to help participants advance to the next level with ease. So, the answer of every level needs to be given in the #answer-here channel in the following format: $diseasename ( $ is the prefix which is followed by the scientific name of the disease, all lowercase with no spaces). For example if the answer to level 0 is Aquagenic Urticaria, you will type $aquagenicurticaria in the #answer-here channel. If your answer is correct, Synapse Bot will tell you that you have received the level 1 role and you will be able to see the level 1 clue. If any team feels that they have the right answer but the bot is not giving the role, the team can get confirmation on their private chat with patient

6. You can only start the event using a special command which will be given to you through the EvolvePD 2020 server on the day of the event.

7. For tests, ask the Patient. The tests have to be specific to an extent. For example, just saying "I want an X-ray conducted" will not work. Instead say "I want an X-ray of the foot...etc". Permission HAS TO BE TAKEN from the patient before conducting any test.

8. DO NOT GIVE ANSWERS OR ASK FOR TESTS ON THE EVOLVEPD 2020 GENERAL SERVER . It will do nothing but put your team at a disadvantage because now the others have received a clue or an answer. It will also result in immediate disqualification of your team.

9.The channels in the ER server are only for doctor-patient interactions, all team discussions must be held elsewhere/on other platforms

10. This is a cryptic hunt so none of the questions are direct. Ask clever questions,  analyse the test results, study your patients' behaviour and you will get the answer.

11. Any team caught cheating would be disqualified immediately. If you have proof that a team has cheated, give it to the Administration.  You will receive a special clue for catching cheaters.

12. We have introduced a new feature this year wherein if you do not receive any response from your patient for more than 7 minutes you are eligible for receiving an extra hint. This is only applicable for upto two extra hints.

13. Hints for any level will be given only on EvolvePD 2020 server. No one will receive hints on private team channels. (Except for extra clues for the 7 minute rule and for those who have caught someone cheating). We will provide clues at our own discretion i.e if we feel the majority need a clue to proceed.

You will be able to see the Main Clue for each level under the "Help Me Doctor " category on your ER when the event starts. Each level's clue will be unlocked only after the previous level has been cleared.

May the best Evolve Doctor win!

Event starts at 2:00PM on 13th November,2020
