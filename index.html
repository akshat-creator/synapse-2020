Transmitting Through You

Synapse' – a multifarious effort by an assemblage of biophilles. Through Synapse, we aim to instil our love of the Sciences in the mass of literati hoarding through our halls, and to help them realise the vast potential held stock within them.
So why Synapse? Why not Mitochondria or some other biological singularity? We chose to call ourselves Synapse because it best describes our ideas. We don’t want to be the power-house of our school, we want to be a medium for exchange of philosophies. We want to connect each student to another, to create an eclectic network for the barter of information, not unlike the intricate connections of a neuron. We want to transmit.
This is just the beginning, the start of something phenomenally mind-staggering – we yearn to take this venture the next step forward, to climb the mountain till we reach the top, and to turn Synapse into the brainchild of all us Mother’s International School apprentices.
