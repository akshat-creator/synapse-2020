FAQS

What is EPD?
EPD, or EvolvePD is the annual event of Synapse, the Biology Club of The Mother’s International School where the participants play the role of a virtual doctor, identifying the disease with the help of various clues and cues given by the patients (played by Synapse Members). 
When is EPD 2020?
EvolvePD is on 11th November. It will start at 2:00 in the afternoon and end at 11:11 in the night of the same day. 
Do I have to do EPD in teams?
Even though it is not compulsory, EPD is best done when in a team. There is so much to explore and do as a virtual doctor, that it will be less fun to do it alone. However, you may register individually, in teams of 2 or maximum of 3. The teams could be from different grades, but it is preferable that students make teams in their own grade.
